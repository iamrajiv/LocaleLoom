## About Bengaluru

Bengaluru, India’s tech capital, blends innovation with tradition. Here’s a quick dive into what makes this city tick (2025).

#### Overview

- **Nickname**: Silicon Valley of India, Garden City.
- **Population**: ~14 million—bustling yet green.
- **Vibe**: IT hubs meet old-school charm—think MG Road’s buzz and Cubbon Park’s calm.
- **Weather**: Mild winters (17-29°C, Oct-Feb), hot summers (up to 36°C, Mar-May).

#### Culture

- **Roots**: A Kannada-speaking heart with a global twist—over 50% of residents are multilingual.
- **Festivals**: Ugadi (Mar-Apr) brings sweets and street cheer; Deepavali lights up markets.
- **Life**: Coffee shops, breweries, and darshanis define the social scene.

#### Learn Kannada Basics

Kannada, the local language, unlocks Bengaluru’s soul.

##### Why Learn?

- Shows respect for local culture.
- Builds rapport with locals—auto drivers soften up.
- Handy for markets, small talk, and rural day trips.

#### Essential Phrases

| English      | Kannada                | Pronunciation  |
| ------------ | ---------------------- | -------------- |
| Hello        | ನಮಸ್ಕಾರ (Namaskāra)    | Na-mas-kaa-ra  |
| Thank you    | ಧನ್ಯವಾದ (Dhanyavāda)   | Dhan-ya-vaa-da |
| How much?    | ಎಷ್ಟು? (Eṣṭu?)         | Esh-tu         |
| Yes          | ಹೌದು (Haudu)           | How-du         |
| No           | ಇಲ್ಲ (Illa)            | Il-la          |
| Where is...? | ಎಲ್ಲಿ ಇದೆ? (Elli ide?) | El-li i-de     |
| Water        | ನೀರು (Nīru)            | Nee-ru         |

#### Tips

- Greet with “Namaskāra”—works any time of day.
- Practice “Eṣṭu?” at KR Market—saves a few rupees.
- Apps like _Duolingo_ or _Kannada Baruthe_ help beyond basics.

#### Quick Fact

Bengaluru’s name comes from “Bendakaḷūru” (boiled beans)—a legend says a king was fed here centuries ago.
