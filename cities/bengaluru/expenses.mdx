#### Living Costs in Bengaluru

Bengaluru’s expenses vary by lifestyle (2025 estimates).

#### Housing

- **1BHK Rent**: ₹15,000-₹25,000 (Koramangala, Indiranagar); ₹8,000-₹15,000 (Whitefield).
- **PG**: ₹6,000-₹12,000/month with food and utilities.
- **Deposit**: 2-10 months’ rent.

#### Food

- **Local Eateries**: ₹50-₹100/meal (dosas, idlis at darshanis).
- **Restaurants**: ₹300-₹500/meal mid-range.
- **Groceries**: ₹3,000-₹5,000/month for one; ₹5,000-₹8,000 for two.

#### Transport

- **Monthly Pass**: ₹1,500-₹2,000 (metro/bus).
- **Auto (Daily)**: ₹200-₹400.
- **Fuel**: ₹5,000-₹8,000/month for personal vehicles.

#### Utilities

- **Electricity/Water**: ₹500-₹1,500/month.
- **Internet**: ₹700-₹1,200/month (100 Mbps).

#### Budget Hacks

- Share PG/rent to cut costs.
- Cook 3-4 times/week—saves ₹2,000+.
